External Programs

Jarvis Includes many external utilities all to help you speed up your workflow.

Note : As these are external programs, so they cannot be troubleshooted and are opened in their own seperate window.

  1. Number System Convertor

    number system convertor

    To open a powerful number system convertor

    Convert any number with any base system to any base system.

    Example - easily convert binary numbers to hexa, hexa to decimals and vice versa.

  2. Random Generator

    generate random

    To open a powerful generator which can generate anything from strings to numbers randomly

  3. Coin Toss

    coin toss

    Apart from the built in simple coin tossing tool, Jarvis also includes a additional advanced version of coin toss.

    In this you can get the result with upto 100 degree of randomness.

  4. Group Generator

    group generator

    Many times in school you have situations where you want to make groups of students, Now Jarvis also as a tool for that.

    Just enter the roll no's of the students (enter 100-110 to input quickly from 100 to 110) and them program will generate a random list of groups.

  5. Interest Calculator

    interest calculator

    To open a powerful interest calculator

    Get the accurate interest rate results between any two dates.

  6. Average Finder

    average

    To open a powerful Average Finder

    Just keep entering numbers, programm= will automatically count them and show you the average of all.
